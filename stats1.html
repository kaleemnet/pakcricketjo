This Financial Tracking System (FTS) is based on funds received by refugee response partners in Jordan. It only covers the inter-agency appeal for the refugee pillar of the 3RP, in support of the Jordan Response Plan (JRP). The JRP FTS remains the authoritative source for funds pledged and allocated to the Jordan Resilience Plan (JRP) log-frame. For more information, see http://www.jrpsc.org/financial-tracking/ .
